Ingredients

  • 400g pasta (spaghetti or linguine)
  • 4 eggs
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Black pepper

Method

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Mix eggs, cream and parmesan
  3. Stir into cooked pasta, and return to the pan for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Serve with black pepper and extra parmesan.
